# Books

Long-form introductions to the EconIAC framework, written for practitioners.
Each book develops a single theme from worked examples upward — no prior mathematics required.

---

## [The Topology of Risk](../book_topology_of_risk/index.md)

*Identifying, Measuring, and Controlling Inconsistency in Financial Systems*

Eight chapters from double-entry bookkeeping as $\partial^2 = 0$ to the 2008 crisis as an $H^2$ event.
Aimed at risk managers, XVA desks, regulators, and fund managers who want the mathematical
foundations without category theory or spectral sequences.
